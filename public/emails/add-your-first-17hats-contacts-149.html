You can add Contacts a variety of different ways with 17hats. I am going to take you through the quickest and easiest way.

Creating a Contact:

  1. Click Contact from the left sidebar.
  2. Click the Plus button next to Contacts.
  3. Select Basic Contact Form from the dropdown menu. This selection is the easiest.
  4. Add the Contacts info in the dialogue box.

Advanced Tips:

Tags: I'll be chatting with you more about Tags as we go along. For now, it's good to know that Tags can be added to Contacts and Projects. I would suggest two Tags for your Contacts, for starters:

  • The year that the Contact first inquired about your service.
  • How they heard about you: e.g., Facebook, Instagram, referral

While going "Tag crazy" might be tempting, it's not a great idea. Keeping your Tags simple is the way to go. As your journey progresses into an automation level, you will be able to store more specific information in Custom Fields.

Adding Contact Details: You can add birthdays, multiple email addresses, and phone numbers - even your Contact's social media handles.

  • Once the Contact is created, select Edit Contact from the top right hand corner to add or make changes.
  • Or, add your details from the beginning. When adding a Contact, select Full Contact Form from the dropdown, and add all your information from the start.
