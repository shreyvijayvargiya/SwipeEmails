HOW TO PLAY
GRID MASTER

Members and F2P players - log into a world marked Grid Master and start your journey.

Tackle the grid tile by tile, in any order to earn rewards and Grid Points.

Spend your Grid Points in the reward shop once Grid Master has ended.

NOT A MEMBER YET?

There are seven tiles available for non-members, so you can still get involved! To access the full game and earn even more rewards, become a member today and take on the entire grid in all its criss-crossed glory.

From grid-inspired FashionScape items to an iconic Chapel transformation, these cosmetic rewards can be won only by playing Grid Master. Simply claim them on the main OSRS worlds with the Grid Points you win, choose your colourways, and display your goods!
