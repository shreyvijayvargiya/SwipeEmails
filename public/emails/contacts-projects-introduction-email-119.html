Today, let's get familiar with Contacts and Projects, the center of the action for your 17hats account.

Projects are where the magic starts for each client. The project is your central organization hub where you can manage important details and view client specific documents, notes and to-do's at a glance.

Every project you create will be associated with a Contact. Think of the relationship between Projects and Contacts like folders in a file cabinet. Contacts are the hanging pendaflex folders you have in your file cabinet. Projects are the manilla folders that organize the documents you drop inside.

If you're ready to work with clients today, go ahead and add in a real Contact and start building out your very first Project right now.
